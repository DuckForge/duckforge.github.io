We're forging two games right now

1. Just Keep Looting

Crawl the corridors of a hidden dungeon below the local pub. Kill monsters, keep the good loot and sell the unwanted stuff for a profit at the local shop keeper. Just Keep Looting is available to wishlist on Steam and will be released soon.

2. Many Ducks

Take the role of a cat who takes over an abandoned duck ranch in the charming town of Waddleton Cove, where ducks are companions, performers, and sometimes even a little magical.

As the new duck rancher, you will raise and train a diverse flock to compete in the town’s annual Duck Parade, a festival where only the most dazzling and unique ducks take center stage. Along the way, you will explore a vibrant world, befriend quirky townsfolk, and uncover duck-related mysteries.
