About Duck Forge

Duck Forge is a passionate indie game studio dedicated to crafting unique, immersive, and charming gaming experiences. Our small team is committed to delivering games that capture hearts, minds, and imaginations.

Our Projects

Many Ducks is an enchanting life simulation RPG where you play as a cat managing a bustling duck ranch in a vibrant 2.5D world. With procedurally generated landscapes and AI-powered interactions, every adventure feels fresh and unique.

As you prepare for the town's cherished Duck Parade festival, you'll hatch, raise, and evolve extraordinary ducks, exploring diverse terrains and bonding with quirky townsfolk.

Meet the Duck Forge Team

Our talented and dedicated team of developers, artists, and writers is excited to bring Many Ducks to life. We're driven by a shared passion for creativity, storytelling, and, of course, ducks!
