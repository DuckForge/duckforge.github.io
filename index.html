Our Values

Commitment

Community

Creativity

Commitment can mean a lot of things to a lot of people. To us, it means doing what you say you're going to do. No passing the buck, no excuses. We get it done. When things turn out the way they're supposed to, there's another important word we use to represent that: quality.


Commitment is a two way street. Just as we ask our team to commit to getting things done, we as a studio make a commitment to them. We are committed to trusting, respecting, and valuing our team. As long as the work is getting done, it doesn't matter how you work, when you work, or where you work. Results are what matter.

We talk a lot about Community at Duck Forge. We, as a team, are our own community. We, as individuals, are part of many different communities. From the groups that define us, to the areas we live in. These are all our communities and they are all important.


We seek to provide representation for communities and provide them a voice because through diversity we gain strength. The more voices we have at the table, the stronger we can speak.

Everyone is creative: fact. We respect the creativity that everyone brings to the table, from artists to programmers. We believe in nurturing the creativity in everyone because through creativity comes passion, and through passion comes success. We want everything we do to be driven by creativity and passion, because when you are excited and passionate about what you're doing, you don't need any extra motivation to do your best work. If everyone is doing their best work, well that's when the magic happens.
